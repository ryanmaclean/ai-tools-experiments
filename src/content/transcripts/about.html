What is AI Tools Lab?

AI Tools Lab consists of 30-40 minute recorded technical demonstrations of hands-on (early or first) experiences with AI-related tooling for developers. Each episode explores different aspects of AI tools and their practical applications in software development.

Why AI Tools Lab?

Our goal is to help teams navigate the AI era by leading discussions, demos, and observations that are grounded in curiosity and honest experiences. By sharing these insights, we aim to be leaders, not laggers, through this technological transition.

What it means to be a developer is changing rapidly. Therefore, what it means to be a developer advocate is changing rapidly as well. Through AI Tools Lab, we explore these changes together.

Our Goals

  1. Education and Exploration: To provide clear, concise technical demonstrations of AI tools, focusing on practical applications for developers.
  2. Honest Assessment: To offer grounded, real-world perspectives on the capabilities and limitations of these tools based on early experiences.
  3. Community Engagement: To foster discussion and share learning experiences within the developer community about navigating the AI landscape.
  4. Adaptability: To continuously explore new tools and techniques as the AI field evolves, keeping developers informed.

Structure of Each Episode

  1. Introduction: Brief overview of the tool and the episode's focus.
  2. Demonstration: Hands-on walkthrough of setup, core features, and a specific use case.
  3. Observations: Discussion of findings, challenges, potential benefits, and limitations.
  4. Conclusion: Summary of key takeaways and potential next steps.
